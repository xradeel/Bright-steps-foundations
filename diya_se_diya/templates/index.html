Empowering Children through Education. Fostering Social Impact.

Bright Steps Foundation (BSF) is a non-profit educational organization committed to responding to the educational needs of the most disenfranchised and neglected members of civil society. We foster social impact through educating, inspiring, and empowering children and youth to overcome poverty and reach their full potential.
